Functional Surfcraft

About Sancho

Sancho Surfboards is a non profit project. It's goal is to design and build better surfboards, and continue to do so with every iteration developed. The objective of this website is to portray the present development of design and construction of a purely functional Surfboard.

R&D

The research and development is a continuous process. Each iteration is aimed to be better than the previous one. Diferent Surfboard design theories were tested on every iteration made. Every iteration we make we are closer to a "perfect" surfboard. The objective is to make a simple and purely functional surfboard, that has all the features needed and nothing else is added. Right now is the 9th iteration of the surfboard, hence Prototype9.

Prototype9

The surfboard that is currently being built is the Prototype 9 (P9), is the most refined iteration so far. It implements various design theories such as the planing hull and sidecut theory. Also some design elements that were uniquely developed for Sancho during the life of the project are implemented. Construction is being made with EPS core, Basalt, innegra, glass fibers and epoxy resin. It aims to be flexible and durable.
